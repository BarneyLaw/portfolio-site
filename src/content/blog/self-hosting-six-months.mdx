Six months ago I went all-in on self-hosting. Here's the honest accounting: the
services I kept, the ones I quietly dropped, and the one that nearly bricked my
NAS.

## Kept

Vaultwarden, Immich, and a Forgejo instance earned their keep immediately. They
replaced subscriptions I was actively paying for and the migration was painless.

## Dropped

A self-hosted RSS reader and a Matrix homeserver both got cut — not because
they were bad, but because maintaining them cost more attention than they
returned.
